    The Nepal Women's Project is non-for-profit 501(c)3 organization I co-founded in March 2018 with the goal of making health and education accessible to women in rural Nepal. I was primarily responsible for the outreach and online presence of the organization; I managed the social media accounts and website, as well as generating content (photographs, text, video) of the organization's activities to be posted online.

    What I learned:

    Web design, Wordpress, web hosting, photography, logo design, brand identity design, merchandising, fundraising.
