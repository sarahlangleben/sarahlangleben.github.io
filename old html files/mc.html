    This is an ongoing project I am doing on behalf of the McCombe Foundation and the German Consulate General of New York City. I am responsible for organizing, documenting, and archiving photographs taken by the photographer Leonard McCombe during and after World War II. I am also helping to plan and organize an exhibition in collaboration with the German Consulate General of New York City to showcase the photographs.

    What I learned:

    Adobe Lightroom, how to work with film photography, exhibition/event planning, document preservation and storage, audio transcription.
